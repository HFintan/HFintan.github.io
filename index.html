About me

Caveat lector: This site is in woeful need of proper updating. It's on the to-do list.

Having completed a Ph.D. in Mathematics and a B.Sc. in Financial Mathematics and Economics in National University of Ireland, Galway, followed by 20 months as a postdoctoral research fellow in the Theoretical and Computational Optimization Research Group at University of Birmingham, I have been working remotely (based in Galway, Ireland) as a production editor for Mathematical Sciences Publishers, University of California, Berkeley, since 2016, and as a production manager since 2020.

I am currently acting manager of the centre for Support for Undergraduate Mathematics and Statistics (SUMS) in National University of Ireland, Galway, to cover sabbatical leave.
I also lecture Mathematics on NUIG's Access diplomas, and teach NUIG's final year "Teaching and Learning in Mathematics" module.
