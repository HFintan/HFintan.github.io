About me
Truncated octahedron

Having completed a Mathematics PhD in computational mathematics and a BSc. in Financial Maths and Economics in the National University of Ireland, Galway, followed by 20 months as a postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Theoretical and Computational Optimization Research Group at the University of Birmingham, and a couple of months lecturing for the University of Limerick, I am now working as a production editor for Mathematical Sciences Publishers, Berkeley. Check out my Research pages for further information on my PhD and postdoctoral work.
