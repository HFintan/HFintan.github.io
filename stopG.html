      • Tinder Calculator


      The calculator gives an estimate of the number of matches you might expect to receive for a given number of swipes, in a given Tinder population.
      Caveats:
      The calculator assumes everyone swipes right on everyone.
      It also does not take Tinder's actual algorithm into account, because we don't know what it is. It assumes that other users are shown to you at random - ie. none of this "friends of friends" or "similar interests" lark, no preference given to people who have already swiped on you, and no psychological inferences about your attractiveness based on your interactions with other users - just an assumption of complete randomness, and cold, flaccid mathematics. However, if your calculations here lead you to believe that you are more attractive than you felt heretofore, then more power to you. For further details, see here.
